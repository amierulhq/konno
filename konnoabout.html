Owner. Visionary Behind It All.

Fealianty Alisya Law

"Konno is a vibrant and emerging small business that I, Fealianty Alisya Law, proudly founded in 2024 in Kuching, Malaysia. As a full-time student with a deep passion for desserts, I decided to turn my love for indulgent treats into something special. Konno offers a diverse selection of delectable desserts and refreshing sweet drinks that are designed to satisfy the cravings of anyone with a sweet tooth. Every item on our menu is crafted with care, using high-quality ingredients and unique flavor combinations to appeal to both local and international palates. What started as a small idea quickly grew into a beloved destination for dessert lovers in Kuching. We are dedicated to delivering not only the best desserts but also a delightful and memorable experience for every customer who walks through our doors. Through our hard work and commitment to excellence, Konno has become a place where people can enjoy both the sweetness of our creations and the warmth of the atmosphere. I am truly grateful to see the support we’ve received and excited to continue growing Konno into a cherished part of this vibrant community."
